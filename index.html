Love in Action, Hope in Every Child


Welcome to Eazy-Ira Foundation

Eazy-Ira Foundation is a charitable organisation established by group of Students from Adventist University of Central, committed to bringing love, education, and spiritual growth to vulnerable children and their families.

What We Do

Child Support

Providing food, clothes, Bible stories and emotional care to children in need.

Education

Supporting access to quality education for every child we reach.

Spiritual Growth

Sharing the message of hope and the love of Jesus with children.

Family Empowerment

Helping families build sustainable futures hope and share Jesus Love.
